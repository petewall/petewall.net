I am a software engineer who loves to work with people to make great things. I have experience at small companies creating web apps and microprocessor firmware; at medium companies creating VR experiences and IRB-approved research projects; and at large enterprises creating robust and reliable software.

I love open source: Using great tools is cool. Creating great tools for others is better! Whether at work, or on personal projects, I want to contribute quality code to benefit everyone.

This website was made for two reasons: First, make a place for me to document the projects I work on, which is a good exercise in keeping things clear and concise. Second, keep a record of challenges I encounter, potentially helping others navigate the same or similar issues.
